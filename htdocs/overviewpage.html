Drainage systems are designed to carry excess rainwater and wastewater away from roads, homes, and public spaces. However, in Butuan City, many drainage canals and waterways have become clogged with trash, sediment, and debris. This reduces the flow of water and leads to flooding during heavy rains.
KEY FACTS:
  • Butuan experiences heavy rainfall especially during rainy seasons, making proper drainage very important.

  • Many drainage channels across the city are open, making them more exposed to waste dumping.

  • Clogged drains affect both urban barangays and low-lying residential areas.
