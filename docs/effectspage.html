HOW CLOGGED DRAINS AFFECT THE COMMUNITY

  1. Frequent Flooding

    2. Widespread destruction of property and infrastructure, loss of life, and long-term economic and health problems like contaminated water supplies.


  2. Health Risks

    3. Promoting the spread of infectious diseases, attracting pests,degrading air quality, and contaminating water sources.


  3. Environmental Pollution

    4. Increased flooding, public health hazards and environmental degradation, leading to overflows that release untreated wastewater.


  4. Economic Impact

    5. Damaging infrastructure, increasing public costs, disrupting businesses, and negatively affecting public health and the environment.
