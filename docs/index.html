Flooding isn’t just caused by the rain. It’s caused by us

Clogged drainage systems in Butuan City continue to cause floods, health risks, and disruptions in our daily lives.

As Butuan City continues to grow and urbanize, our drainage systems have become more vulnerable to clogging caused by waste, debris, and sediment buildup. When drains are blocked, rainwater can no longer flow properly, leading to flooding in streets, residential areas, and public spaces.


This website aims to raise awareness about the issue, explain its causes and effects, and offer practical ways that both the community and local government can help prevent clogged drains. Together, we can work toward a cleaner and flood-resilient Butuan City.
